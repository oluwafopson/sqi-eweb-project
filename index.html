sqi scholarship

Study at SQI at little or NO cost!

In 2011 SQI College of ICT, then known as SQI ICT Academy, launched a yearly scholarship program to open a wider window of opportunity to every promising individual who is passionate about acquiring an ICT skill, and today we have trained and certified over 7000+ youths who currently hold important and viable positions in various organizations nationwide.

The scholarship program is open to every individual between the ages of 13 to 35. This annual scholarship program gives everyone (i.e O-level, A-Level, School Leavers, Undergraduates, Employed Graduates, Unemployed Graduates etc.) an opportunity to take any of our ICT courses at little or no cost.

The scholarship program has over the years taken many unique approaches to identify and evaluate suitable candidates for the award of either of full of Partial scholarship.

Who is eligible?

The scholarship program is open to every individual between ages 13 to 35. This annual scholarship program gives everyone (i.e O-level, A-Level, School Leavers, Em ployed Graduates, Unemployed Graduates e.tc) an opportunity to take any of our computer courses at little or no cost.

Our Target Groups
  • School leavers who wish to acquire demonstrable practical skills to secure employment or generate employment.
  • Persons seeking career paths that do not need university degrees.
  • Persons who have not studied for some time and desire to do so.
  • Persons without time for full-time study but want to enhance their skills.
  • Persons wishing to go into self-employment.
  • University graduates seeking employable skills and adults seeking opportunities to re-skill themselves.

TERMS & CONDITIONS

  • The scholarship test is over 100. The candidate’s score determines the discount that the candidate will enjoy.
  • To qualify for a full scholarship, a candidate must score 76% and above.
  • A candidate who scores below 76% will qualify for a discount-based scholarship.
  • Candidates that score below 40% will be compensated with a 20% discount.
  • This year’s scholarship test shall be conducted on-site and online at our campuses in Ogbomoso, Ibadan, and Abeokuta (See flier for addresses).
FULL SCHOLARSHIP TERMS

a. Only the first 10 persons to score 76% and above in this year’s test will be considered for the full scholarship (i.e. it is on a first-come, first-serve basis.)

b. Full scholarship is applicable only to one level. (i.e, a candidate will take the course of choice for a level absolutely FREE)

c. After the first level, the candidate will be required to proceed on percentage-based payment (i.e. a discounted payment on subsequent levels, based on the candidate’s score.)

DISCOUNT-BASED SCHOLARSHIP TERMS

a. Any candidate below 76% will be considered for the discount-based scholarship.b. The scholarship is percentage-based, that is, the percentage score of a participant will be deducted from the sum total of what he or she is required to pay.

b. Any candidate who scores below 40 percent will be compensated with a 20 percent discount.

c. The discount-based scholarship is only applicable to the first three levels. This means that the discount cannot be applied for level 4 and above of any course.

PAYMENT TERMS

a. All candidates are expected to pay a non-refundable acceptance fee of six thousand Naira (₦ 6, 000) not later than one (1) week after the scholarship result is out. Failure to do this shall result in the forfeiture of the scholarship.



N.B:

i. The acceptance fee is for the acknowledgment of the scholarship offered.

ii. The ₦6,000 acceptance fee validates your scholarship status only till the 30th of September, 2023. This is the deadline for payment of the course fee. Failure to pay the discounted course fee by this date will lead to losing the scholarship.

b. Participants are expected to make full payment of the discounted course fee before the commencement of the training.

c. Kindly note that part payment is not accepted for the course payment.

OTHER TERMS AND CONDITIONS.

a. All candidates for this year’s scholarship will commence classes on the 7th of August, 2023 for their first cohort, therefore candidates are enjoined to make their payments on time to join the first cohort, else, once the class limit is filled, participants will be drafted for the next cohort which starts on the 4th of September, 2023.

b. Candidates that desire to use the scholarship discount to study the course of their choice must have completed their course on or before the 31st of May, 2024 regardless of whether the course was deferred or not. Failure to do so means the scholarship discount will be revoked and candidates will need to complete the payment for their course which the discount had covered earlier.

c. Should a candidate desire to defer their course to a later period, notification of absence or deferment of training must be done in writing to the office of the Rector, for proper consideration and approval.

d. The scholarship can be applied to one (1) course only. Should the candidate decide to register for more than one course, the candidate will be required to pay for the other course in full.

e. The scholarship discount can only be applied to the first three levels and it cannot be applied to courses for level 4 and above.

f. Successful candidates are to conduct themselves well as ambassadors of the college. Any behavior that violates the values of the college within or outside the campus premises may result in the forfeiture of the scholarship.

g. A prospective candidate can only take the test once during the scholarship period. Any candidate who tries to take the test more than once will be disqualified from the scholarship program.

h. Any candidate caught involved in malpractice during the scholarship test shall be disqualified.

i. Only those within the age range of 13-35 years old are eligible for the scholarship.

j. Kindly note that other bonuses or discounts cannot be combined with this scholarship discount.
